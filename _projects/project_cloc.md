The CLOC-study is a randomized, parallel-group treatment, quadruple masked, two-arm study to assess the effectiveness of cod liver oil compared to placebo in the prevention of COVID-19 and airway infections in healthy adults. The study has approx. 35 000 participants. (ClinicalTrials.gov Identifier: NCT04609423)
